Crop Classification

Issue

  • Accurate classification and monitoring of crops are essential for efficient agricultural planning and management

Solution

  • Approach:
    Develop AI models to classify different crop types and monitor their growth stages.
  • Method:
    Utilize remote sensing data and machine learning algorithms to analyze spectral signatures and temporal patterns of crops. Implement multispectral and hyperspectral imaging to differentiate between various crop species and identify growth stages.
  • Outcome:
    Enable precise crop classification and monitoring, assisting farmers in making informed decisions about planting, irrigation, and harvesting, ultimately enhancing crop productivity and resource management.
