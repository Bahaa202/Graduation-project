Climate Change

Issue

  • Climate change induces erratic weather patterns and extreme events, profoundly impacting crop productivity and agricultural operations.

Solution

  • Approach:
    Employ LSTM models within a time series framework to forecast critical climatic variables such as temperature, humidity, and wind speed.
  • Method:
    Train the LSTM models using extensive historical climate data sourced from the NASA Power website, spanning a decade.
  • Outcome:
    Deliver precise climate forecasts that enable proactive farming strategies, enhancing resilience against unpredictable weather conditions.
